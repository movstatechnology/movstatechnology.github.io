About us

Mission

MOVSTA Technology Pvt.Ltd is a Technology driven startup working on Innovative product and service with 100% made in India essence. The Startup in created to encourage the Indian Innovators and scientists to come under one roof and make best in class product for our own people.

Vision

We are looking forward to providing the best cutting-edge technology and educational services to people of India with the most affordable price range. We focus on developing product which should reach the last man of our country and help everyone to be digitally connected with technological awareness.
